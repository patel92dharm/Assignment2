Quick Facts

Quick Facts
Full Name Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani
Date of Birth April 19, 1957
Occupation Chairman of Reliance Industries

Mukesh Ambani, the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries Limited, is one of the world's most influential businessmen. Under his leadership, Reliance has grown into a global powerhouse in energy, petrochemicals, telecommunications, and retail. He has been instrumental in the creation of Reliance Jio, a revolutionary telecom network that transformed India's digital landscape.

A visionary leader, Mukesh Ambani has consistently ranked among the wealthiest individuals worldwide. His contributions extend beyond business, with significant investments in education, healthcare, and sustainability through the Reliance Foundation. His focus on innovation and customer-centric solutions has set benchmarks in multiple industries.
